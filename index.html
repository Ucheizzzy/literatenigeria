About Literate Nigeria

  • Who we are
  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision
Literate Nigeria is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting education and academic excellence in Nigeria. Our aim is to improve the educational standards of Nigerian students by providing them with the necessary resources and support to succeed in their academic pursuits
Our mission at Literate Nigeria is to empower Nigerian students with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in their academic pursuits, and to promote the importance of education as a tool for national development.
Our vision at Literate Nigeria is to create a future where every Nigerian student has access to quality education, and is equipped with the necessary knowledge and skills to become a productive member of society.
