About Literate Nigeria

Literate Nigeria is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting education and academic excellence in Nigeria. Our aim is to improve the educational standards of Nigerian students by providing them with the necessary resources and support to succeed in their academic pursuits
Our mission at Literate Nigeria is to empower Nigerian students with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in their academic pursuits, and to promote the importance of education as a tool for national development.
Our vision at Literate Nigeria is to create a future where every Nigerian student has access to quality education, and is equipped with the necessary knowledge and skills to become a productive member of society.

Scholarship For Young Ones

Literate Nigeria is dedicated to supporting the education of young Nigerian students who have demonstrated exceptional academic talent and commitment. We offer scholarships solely on the basis of merit, giving students the chance to pursue their academic goals without any financial burden. In addition to providing scholarships, Literate Nigeria offers mentorship and support to our scholarship recipients. We believe in nurturing the academic potential of our students and providing them with the resources they need to succeed. Our scholarship program is open to students from diverse backgrounds across Nigeria, giving equal opportunities to all. We are committed to making a positive impact on the Nigerian society by investing in education, which we believe is the key to building a better future. By investing in the education of these young students, we aim to cultivate a new generation of Nigerian leaders who will contribute positively to the country's development. Join us on this journey towards a brighter future for Nigeria by applying for our scholarships today. Let us work together to empower and educate the next generation of Nigerian leaders.

Heroes And Knights

Literate Nigeria is willing to recognize individuals who performed exceptionally well during their time of service in a variety of industries, including education, health care, agriculture, defense, fashion, banking, tourism, oil and gas, entertainment, etc. and deserve recognition. This is because loyalty and excellent performance are important to us. Have you worked with or do you know someone who you firmly believe deserves recognition for the services they provided while they were actively in service and you want to celebrate? The retired professional is eligible to be nominated for an award and a documentary about their life while in service. We ask that you make that nomination now

Train The Trainers

Literate Nigeria is committed to promoting education as a tool for national development in Nigeria. Our mission is to empower Nigerian students with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in their academic pursuits while also promoting the importance of education in the country's development. Our programs are designed to help teachers acquire new skills, knowledge, and teaching methodologies that will enable them to provide high-quality education to their students. We also provide teachers with access to the latest technologies and teaching tools to help them enhance their teaching methods. Additionally, our courses and training programs are not only for current teachers but also for those interested in switching to a new career path or acquiring new knowledge. We understand the importance of continuous learning and development in any profession, which is why we welcome anyone with a passion for teaching and education to take advantage of our courses. By empowering teachers with the tools they need to succeed, we are creating a brighter future for Nigerian students and contributing to the growth and development of our country. We believe that education is the key to unlocking the potential of Nigeria's youth and driving national development. Therefore, we will continue to invest in the professional development of teachers to create a generation of well-trained educators who can transform the education system in Nigeria.

Community Good Deeds

Organizing community service and performing good deeds are essential for building strong and supportive communities. Community service refers to any volunteer work that benefits the local community or society as a whole. Community service allows organisations to make positive impact on the community and society as a whole, whether by helping to clean up a local park, organizing a fundraiser for a charitable works, or simply lending a hand to someone in need. At Literate Nigeria, doing our fair share of good deeds to further improve living conditions is one social obligation we take seriously in an effort to build strong relationships with members of our community and to reinforce those that already exists. Over time, we will devote much more time to charitable endeavors, improve our neighborhoods, and give people more reasons to respect and believe in who we really are.
